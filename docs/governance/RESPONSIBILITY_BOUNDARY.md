# Governance Responsibility Boundary

Version: v0.7.0

## Ultra Brain boundary

Ultra Brain-only Constitution, Convention, Principle, Top Rule, Top Policy, Top Standard, Top Validation, and Top Trigger are outside the authority and implementation scope of OS Ecosystem.

OS Ecosystem does not create, replace, reinterpret, or present itself as the authority for those documents.

## OS Ecosystem responsibility

OS Ecosystem owns only its operating boundary:

- connected OS System registration and navigation
- Capability registration and public contracts
- repository-owned AI Hub lifecycle
- compatibility, validation, release, and deployment evidence
- public Metadata, Route, Status, and Health contracts
- protection of connected-project autonomy and data ownership

## Connected project autonomy

Living OS and Universal Learning Engine retain business rules, data, runtime decisions, versions, and independent deployment. AI Hub remains an OS Ecosystem repository component.
